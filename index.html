About Me

Shawn Michael Yandall-Knudsen. Son to Ake Yandall of Samoa, and Garry Lee Knudsen of Minnesota. I was born and raised in San Diego, California. For the better part of the last decade I have been in the service industry making cocktails for countless patrons. I have been an artist over different mediums throughout my life. Graffiti, as a young adult, always interested me. I'd often take crayons to the wall; Cartoons and action movies inspired my ideas. This interest in character art and handstyles would follow me throughout my life. I am big fan of Hip-Hop and Reggae music... But not so much the modern iterations. I like to keep it traditional. I have lived in Virginia for the last 3 years. It is a beautiful place, with actual weather and seasons. Something a native San Diegan isn't too used to!
